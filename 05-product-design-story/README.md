# 05 - Product Design Story

This folder turns the Marginalia TV prototype into a portfolio-ready product story. It is meant to be read after the research, competitive analysis, PRD/evals, and prototype folders.

## Recommended Reading Order

1. `product-design-framing.md` - the compressed Goal -> JTBD -> Pain -> Options -> MVP -> Metrics spine.
2. `portfolio-case-study.md` - the narrative version for portfolio or interview use.
3. `prototype-walkthrough.md` - how the built prototype maps to the user journey.
4. `decision-log.md` - key product tradeoffs and why they were made.
5. `metrics-and-eval-readout.md` - what the evals proved, what they did not prove, and what to do next.

## One-Line Story

Marginalia TV tests whether returning viewers need more than a plot recap: they need an episode-bounded reconstruction of the story state, including relationships, secrets, power shifts, and ready-to-watch memory anchors.

## Prototype Scope

- MVP show: *The Boys*
- Primary boundary: watched through `S04E08`; preparing for `S05E01`
- Seeded experience: demo login, bookshelf, boundary selector, season recap, episode timeline, relationship map, character status, secrets/power table, ready-to-watch checklist, exit survey
- Explicit non-goal: broad TV tracking, generic AI recap generation, video recaps, speculation, or wiki replacement

## Best Portfolio Angle

Lead with the product judgment: I narrowed a crowded "AI recap" space into a sharper wedge, built a working prototype, and used evals to separate UI readiness from the harder generation/data-quality risk.
